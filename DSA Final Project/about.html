About HomeAlloc

Transparent, Fair, and Efficient Housing Allocation System

Our Mission

To provide fair and transparent housing allocation to families in need through a priority-based system that eliminates bias and ensures equitable distribution of housing resources.

We believe every family deserves a safe and secure home, and our system is designed to make that happen through data-driven, ethical allocation processes.

How Our System Works

1

Application Submission

Families submit their details including age, family size, income, and current housing situation.

2

Priority Calculation

Our algorithm calculates a priority score based on multiple factors to ensure fairness.

3

Review & Verification

Applications are reviewed by our team for accuracy and eligibility.

4

House Allocation

When houses become available, they are allocated based on priority scores and family needs.
